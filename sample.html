The Games Within the Games

Elevate your fandom to the next level with Upside! With both web and mobile platforms, Upside offers unique fantasy challenges around all your favorite college sports. Support your university's alumni association, booster club, athlete unions and more with games and tournaments of your favorite student athletes!

Keep 'Em Coming Back

From sports bars to Michelin stars, Upside gives your customers another reason to return. Select unique redeemable prizes, and give your patrons custom games to play to take engagement to the next level.

Liven Up Your Workplace

Nothing boosts employee morale and engagement like play-to-win competitions. Give your office something to talk about - and encourage participation with unique prizes that you choose!

Do More for Your Organization

Clubs, unions, associations - whatever the reason for getting together, get together better with Upside! Let us set up games and tournament for your organization, and see engagement soar!

The Advantages of Upside

Partnership

Teaming with Upside lets you engage your students, customers, employees, or even friends in creative news ways. Let us build your fantasy challenges, or create your own!

Entertainment

You already love the sports, the teams, the players. Upside helps you create fun and exciting challenges, leveling up the game day experience for your target users.

Engagement

With endless possibilities for game day challenges or season-long tournaments, Upside will keep your target users coming back week after week. You provide the prizes, we provide the fun!
