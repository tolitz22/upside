Upside: Creating the User Experience

Challenges

Create custom challenges for your targeted base. Prizes are custom, too -- make your own reward token (featured), offer products/services, even meet & greets with VIPs. Everything is customizable -- if you can think it, we can do it! Upside can also provide templates for our more popular challenges.

User Experience

Users can expand their challenge view, use sorting and filters, and edit various profile setting to ensure they have the perfect experience.

Tournaments

Upside also supports tournament play! Tournaments, especially those with daily or weekly challenges, are a perfect way to keep your users coming back.

Gameplay

You set the number of picks a user makes, the type of picks, the challenge descriptions -- all of it! Users can easily drag and drop, delete, select, and submit picks from our easy-to-use interface.

Picks Lists

If you're a college or university, or a sports team at any level, this is a great place to feature your own athletes! Backgrounds and pics are customizable, and we support stats displays as well.

Store

If offering token or cash prizes, populate your store with your own merchandise or experiences. The more unique, the more popular with users!
