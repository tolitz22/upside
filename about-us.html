Our Story

Upside launched in 2018 with a single principle: Fantasy sports should be better. Why be restricted to a handful of apps, all with basically the same gameplay? So we made it our mission to bring fantasy to the local level, and put the creativity behind the games into your hands.

Since then, Upside has teamed with universities, sports bars, pro leagues, podcasts, distillers and more to create unique, individualized, homegrown fantasy tournaments and challenges. With custom prizes and seamless gameplay, Upside has helped our partners engage with thousands of users and fans across dozens of industries throughout the US and abroad.

Looking to engage with your base in fun and rewarding way? Let Upside work for you! Reach out to us and let's see if we're a fit.
